SNOW on the BEACH

SEPTEMBER 2, 2023

HYATT REGENCY LAKE TAHOE | LAKESIDE BALLROOM

We are excited to invite you

to the Diamond Peak Foundation’s Second Annual
Après Après Soirée - Snow On The Beach.

Snow On The Beach will be our largest donor party
of the year and will welcome guests from our ski
community to delight in an afternoon filled with
wonderful food, winter white cocktails, and
exciting live music.

This event is your opportunity

to bid on the coveted Diamond Peak Parking Spot. The spot
will go up for auction following dinner, and a few other
exciting items will also be available.

Profits and charitable funds raised will go to further the design
and construction of the Ski Chalet. Currently known as “The Shack”,
located in the north west corner of the top car park at Diamond Peak,
the goal is to construct a new state-of-the-art building that will
include classroom learning, a meal room, coach offices and give
the Foundation a greater opportunity to support the community
with year-round training and camps.

The Diamond Peak Ski Education Foundation is a unique non-profit that provides area athletes with an exceptional opportunity to build strong character through the Diamond Peak Ski Team. More than 225 athletes, ages 5 – 99 and 50 coaches participate in DSPT’s ski program each season.As a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, attracting top coaching talent and providing access for year-round training opportunities, athletes require community support in the form of sponsorship and fundraising. DPSEF awards needs-based scholarships to Incline Village/Crystal Bay youth to improve their access to our ski programs. Visit dpsef.org for more information.
